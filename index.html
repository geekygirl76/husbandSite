Let me make your life better

Dr. David Rosenstein, D.C.

Dr. David S. Rosenstein has been the Official Chiropractor at the Westwood Health and Fitness Center for over 31 years, serving the Pascack Valley Community. With the "golden hands" of a trained fine artist, Dr. Rosenstein gently balances the spine using low force techniques in a non-rushed office environment. The experience is often described as very stress reducing, healing and relaxing. This process allows the body to better deal with its challenges and heal itself. Easy to talk to with straight to the point answers and solutions, Dr. Rosenstein invites you to phone him to discuss your problem. Whether you suffer from back or neck pain, auto, sports and work related injuries or just wish to regain and maintain your health, Chiropractic is the right choice for you! Both Health club members and Non-members are welcome! Typical wait time to see the doctor is usually never more than 0-10 minutes. Most insurances are accepted and home visits are also offered.
